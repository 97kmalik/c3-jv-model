C3 Coalition Context where this site fits in the four-state program

The Clean Corridor Coalition is a $249M EPA Climate Pollution Reduction Grant-funded program deploying zero-emission MHDV charging infrastructure along the I-95 freight corridor from Connecticut to Maryland. Round 1 funds 24 publicly accessible sites (NJ: 9, MD: 8, CT: 6, DE: 1) supporting en-route, overnight, and mixed-use charging. Program targets over the corridor's lifetime include 1.7M residents served (30% in LIDAC communities), coalition-wide reductions of 446 tons NOx, 5.8 tons PM2.5, and 53.9 tons VOC from mobile sources, plus 14.6M MMT CO2e reduced by 2050. Workforce commitments include 400 high-quality jobs for LIDAC members through training partnerships.
